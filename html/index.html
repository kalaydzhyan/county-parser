About Us

We are a team of real estate professionals passionate about systematic approach to investing. As full-time investors, agents and property managers ourselves, we are equipped with a unique combination of skills that will give you an edge in your investment journey. We are currently working in the following areas:

  • Abilene, TX, including properties in 60 miles radius
  • Evansville, IN and Vanderburgh County, IN
  • Any other area in the United States for remote investing

Returns on investment that our clients and us achieve range from 10% annually (completely passive) to 20% (mostly passive rental income) to 100% (active improvement, management, etc).

We are also proud of our positive impact on the local communities and affordable housing, as a good business model is always a win-win for everyone. Quite often, we purchase inhabitable properties and make them livable again, and same is done by our clients and colleagues. We also develop and improve vacant land and then sell it or rent it out to those who would use it.

Our services tailored for you

In the current low-yield environment and potential rise in inflation, we recognize the importance of cash-flowing hard assets. We provide a range of services that will help you achieve your long-term financial goals.

  • 01 We help you purchase or sell investment properties

    We provide professional support at all stages of real estate purchase or sale: traditional real estate agent services (marketing your property and listing on MLS, guiding through contracts, lenders, appraisals, inspections, closings), creating comparative market analysis (CMA), estimating investment returns, forming a team of contractors for your investment firm, etc. We can also sell you one of our turnkey properties or buy a property from you for our own portfolio.

  • 02 We create real estate investment products

    We can create a real estate company as a vehicle for passive income, where you will have 100% ownership and total control over the direction, in which this company moves. The company comes with a rental property, tenants and a team of people servicing them (property manager, handymen, insurance brokers, lenders, etc). Alternatively, we can create a promissory note supported by one of our rental properties as an even more passive solution.

  • 03 Data products for investors, agents and wholesalers

    We create aggregated lists of properties using public data sources, updated weekly. Some of the lists (such us delinquent tax properties) do not exist anywhere else in the industry. They can help investors, agents and wholesalers to perform more efficient off-market campains and pick properties before anyone else knows about them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here is a list of question we frequently get from our clients

  • Can you represent me in a real estate transaction?

    Currently, we can represent our clients in Texas only. However, we may have referrals for trusted agents in other areas (e.g., Southern Indiana and Kentucky). If showings are required, we can do them in 60 miles area around Abilene, TX, and, in exceptional cases, beyond. In any situation, we can guide you through an coming or ongoing real estate transaction without a formal representation.

  • Why Abilene, Evansville, etc.?

    Different areas within the U.S. have different real estate markets. In order to find a good rental cash flow, one has to move away from big cities - places, where house prices are low with respect to rents. This allows us to get superior returns on invested capital. Since these small cities attract less attention, one can find more opportunities in terms of market mispricing, too. Also, different states/localities have different laws and regulations making the same business model viable in one state and risky or impossible in another state.

  • What strategies have you implemented already?

    We bought and rented out single-family and small multi-family houses (turnkey and with rehab), "flipped" and sold houses after renovation, also implemented the BRRRR method. We financed our projects with conventional, commercial and private loans. Currently, we are in the process or wholesaling and flipping land, as well as searching for our first commercial deal.

  • Can I invest remotely?

    Yes, absolutely! In fact, most of our houses were purchased remotely. One can obtain a wealth of information from the local MLS service, county website, Google Street View, inspection report, etc. In fact, staying away from the house helps to focus investor's attention on the cold data instead of getting emotionally attached to the house they viewed in person. The key to success is, of course, a trustworthy team of people, who will do your job on ground. To learn more, one can read the following book: "Long-Distance Real Estate Investing: How to Buy, Rehab, and Manage Out-of-State Rental Properties" by David Greene

  • Do you accept international clients?

    Yes, we do. We currently have several clients from Germany and Russia. The main difference between domestic and international investors is taxation and government compliance. While we do have some experience with investing from abroad, one is highly encouraged to consult with a quialified attorney before attempting a deal as a foreign person in the U.S. The attorney should be familiar with the IRS rules for non-U.S. persons and entities, taxation system in the country of investor's residence and tax treaties between the foreign country and the U.S.
